# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We as members, contributors, and maintainers pledge to make participation in the
Kubera community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age,
body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of
experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity
and orientation.

## Our standards

Examples of behaviour that contributes to a positive environment:

- Showing empathy and kindness toward others
- Respecting differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
- Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Unacceptable behaviour includes harassment, insulting/derogatory comments,
public or private harassment, publishing others' private information, and other
conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behaviour may be
reported to the maintainers at `conduct@example.com` (replace for your fork).
All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org),
version 2.1.
